Smart Seal - Privacy Policy

Camera, Photos, and File Permission

Smart Seal application may request access to the camera and photo storage on your device. We prioritize the privacy and security of our users. This policy outlines the purpose of utilizing the camera and file-picker features within the app for personal use. We are committed to ensuring that your data remains private and is not shared with any third parties.

This section outlines how and why these permissions are used:

1. Purpose of Camera and Photos Access

Smart Seal application requires access to the camera and photo storage to allow users to update profile photo within the app. This feature enables you to capture photos or select existing files from your device for various purposes, such as for personal use.

2. User Consent

Before accessing the camera or photo storage, Smart Seal App will explicitly request your consent. You will be prompted to grant permission during the installation process or when attempting to use features that require these permissions.

3. Revoking Permissions

You have the right to revoke camera and photo access permissions at any time. This can typically be done through your device's settings. However, please note that revoking these permissions may limit certain features within the app that rely on accessing the camera or photo storage.
